Monthly Invoice Delivery System

Developed for PT PLN (Persero) UP3 Gresik, this full-stack automation system is engineered to digitize the distribution of electricity bills. Built using a custom PHP-MVC architecture, the platform manages customer records and automates the dispatch of PDF invoices via SMTP servers.

Core Functionalities

  • Customer CRM: Centralized management of customer IDs and contact data.
  • Activity Auditing: Real-time logging of admin and staff actions for security.
